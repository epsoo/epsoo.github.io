About

My name is Sep van der Spiegel. I am currently working in Arnhem, Netherlands. I like to work in different ways, depending on what suits a project best. Sometimes that’s an interactive game or a physical installation, and sometimes it’s graphic work. I try to work from a strong concept but also find it important to keep my projects light-hearted. I hope to encourage interaction between the viewer and my work.
